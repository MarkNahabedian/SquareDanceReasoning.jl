Hierarchy of Supported Square Dance Formations

These are the formations that are currently supported, and the roles supported for those formations:

  • SquareDanceFormation
    • DancerState
    • EightDancerFormation
      • AfterEightChainOne
      • BeforeEightChain
      • CircleOfEight
      • CompletedDoublePassThru
      • FacingTandemCouples
      • NQuarterTag
        • QuarterTag
        • ThreeQuarterTag
      • SquaredSet
      • WaveOfEight
        • LHWaveOfEight: AllButVeryCenters, Centers, Ends, VeryCenters
        • RHWaveOfEight: AllButVeryCenters, Centers, Ends, VeryCenters
    • FourDancerFormation
      • BackToBackCouples
      • BoxOfFour
        • LHBoxOfFour
        • RHBoxOfFour
      • FacingCouples
      • OneByFourFormation
        • ColumnOfFour: Centers, Ends
        • InvertedLineOfFour: Centers, Ends
        • LineOfFour: Centers, Ends
        • TwoFacedLine: Centers, Ends
        • WaveOfFour
          • LHWaveOfFour: Centers, Ends
          • RHWaveOfFour: Centers, Ends
      • Star: Beaus, Belles
      • TandemCouples
    • TwoDancerFormation
      • BackToBack: Leaders
      • Couple: Beaus, Belles
      • FaceToFace: Trailers
      • MiniWave
        • LHMiniWave: Belles
        • RHMiniWave: Beaus
      • Tandem: Leaders, Trailers
