Supported Square Dance Calls

These are the calls that are currently implemented. They might not be implemented from all formations or all CallerLab programs.

  • AndRoll: role
    • DancerState
  • Balance: role, time
    • DancerState
  • Dosado: role, handedness
    • FaceToFace
    • MiniWave
  • FaceLeft: role, time
    • DancerState
  • FaceRight: role, time
    • DancerState
  • Hinge: role, time
    • MiniWave
  • PartnerHinge: role, time
    • Couple
  • PassThru: role
    • FaceToFace
    • RHMiniWave
  • PullBy: role, handedness
    • FaceToFace
    • MiniWave
  • QuarterIn: role
    • Couple
    • MiniWave
  • QuarterOut: role
    • Couple
    • MiniWave
  • SlideThru: role
    • FaceToFace
  • SquareDanceReasoning._FinishTrade: originalbeau, originalbelle, time
    • RHMiniWave
  • SquareThru: role, handedness, count
    • FacingCouples
    • WaveOfFour
  • StarThru: role
    • FaceToFace
  • StepThru: role
    • MiniWave
  • StepToAWave: role, handedness, time
    • FaceToFace
  • Trade: role, time
    • Couple
    • MiniWave
  • UTurnBack: role
    • Couple
    • DancerState
    • MiniWave
  • _BackToAWave: role, handedness
    • BackToBack
  • _FaceOriginalCorner: role, time
    • DancerState
  • _FaceOriginalPartner: role, time
    • DancerState
  • _GenderedRoll: role, time
    • DancerState
  • _Rest: role, time
    • DancerState
  • _UnStepToAWave: role
    • MiniWave
